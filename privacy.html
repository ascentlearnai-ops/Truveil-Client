Candidate privacy notice

Know what Truveil Secure uses before you join.

Truveil Secure runs only after you enter a session code and consent to the interview verification notice.

Information shared with the interviewer

  • Live transcript text and transcript timing.
  • Microphone health and connection status.
  • Foreground app names and window titles during the active session.
  • Browser host or URL when Windows exposes it to the app.
  • Restricted-target events, such as a known AI tool being detected or closed.

Information not intentionally collected

  • No camera video.
  • No screen recording.
  • No file access.
  • No clipboard collection.
  • No messages or personal document collection.

Audio

Microphone audio is used to create live transcript text. Raw audio is not included in the interviewer report. Temporary fallback audio, when used, is designed to be deleted after transcription or failure handling.

Reports

The interviewer may keep a report with transcript text, session events, notes, timestamps, and review-band context for up to 30 days by default during beta.

Questions or deletion

Contact your interviewer or support@truveil.com for privacy or deletion questions.

This notice explains the beta product behavior and does not replace legal notices from the interviewing organization.
