Candidate privacy notice

Truveil Secure runs only after you consent and join an interview session. It may share live transcript text, foreground app names, window titles, browser URLs or hosts exposed by Windows, and restricted-target events with the interviewer.

Temporary fallback audio is used only for transcription and must be deleted after processing. Truveil does not intentionally read your files, clipboard, messages, or browser history.
