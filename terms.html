Candidate beta terms

Terms for joining a verified interview session.

Truveil Secure helps an interviewer run a consent-based verified interview. It provides interview evidence for human review and does not automatically decide whether you violated policy or whether you should be hired.

Your consent

You must review the in-app notice and consent before a session starts. You can choose not to start or you can end the session, but your interviewer may decide whether the interview can continue without verification.

Best-effort controls

Truveil Secure may try to close or refocus away from known restricted AI tools configured by the interviewer. These controls are best effort and are not guaranteed browser-level blocking.

Appropriate use

Do not attempt to bypass the session policy, interfere with telemetry, or misrepresent your work during an interview.

Questions

If you have questions about the session policy, ask your interviewer before starting.

These beta terms explain product use and do not replace legal notices from the interviewing organization.
