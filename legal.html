Trust & Legal

Candidate verification without camera proctoring.

Truveil Secure is designed to keep the candidate experience simple: enter a code, review the notice, allow microphone transcription, and keep the app open while the interview runs.

Product boundaries

  • No camera.
  • No screen recording.
  • No file or clipboard access.
  • No automatic hiring decision.
  • No hidden candidate-facing risk score.

What the interviewer may see

  • Live transcript text and transcript health.
  • Foreground app/window evidence during the session.
  • Browser host or URL when Windows exposes it.
  • Restricted-site events and timestamps.
  • Final interview report evidence.
