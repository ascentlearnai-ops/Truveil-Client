Plain-language privacy

Verification should never be mysterious.

Truveil Secure is built to be understandable. Here is exactly what it does and does not do during your interview.

Microphone

Used for live transcript

Fallback audio is temporary and deleted after transcription. Raw audio is not included in the report.

No camera

No video proctoring

Truveil Secure does not use your camera or record your screen.

Session focus

Foreground changes are recorded

The recruiter may receive the foreground app name, window title, browser URL or host, and detection source when Windows exposes them.

Restricted sites

Known restricted destinations may close

Truveil uses best-effort desktop controls. It does not claim perfect browser-level blocking.

Your files

Not part of the session

Truveil Secure does not read your personal documents, clipboard, messages, or browser history. It does not make the hiring decision.
